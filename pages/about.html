About Rely Tailors

Crafting Confidence, One Stitch at a Time

At Rely Tailors, we believe that every outfit should be a reflection of your personality. Our tailors design custom garments that fit you flawlessly and elevate your style.

From bespoke suits to elegant alterations, we combine tradition with modern precision. Every stitch, every fold, speaks of our passion.

Meet the Owner

Mr. Arbaz Shahid , the founder and visionary behind Rely Tailors, brings over 25 years of experience in custom tailoring and luxury garment crafting. With a deep-rooted passion for precision stitching, fabric mastery, and elegant designs, Mr. Malik has transformed Rely Tailors into a trusted name known for bespoke suits, ethnic wear, and bridal craftsmanship.

His leadership, customer-first philosophy, and attention to detail have been instrumental in building a brand that stands for trust, style, and exceptional quality.
